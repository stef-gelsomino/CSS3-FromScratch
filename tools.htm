Selectors

  • Tag Selectors (TS)

    • p, div, h1, etc...

  • Class Selectors (CS)

    • .NameOfTheClass

  • ID Selectors (IDS)

    • #ID

  • Nasted Selectors (NS)

    • Examples: ul li, .NameOfTheClass p, etc...

Pseudo Classes

  • First Child

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS :first-child {attribute}

  • Last Child

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS:last-child {attribute}

  • Nth Child

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS:nth-child(odd or even or a number) {attribute}

  • First Of Type

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS:first-of-type {attribute}

  • Last Of Type

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS:last-of-type {attribute}

  • Nth Of Type

    • TS or CS or IDS or NS:nth-of-type(odd or even or a number) {attribute}

  • Adjacent Sibling

    • +

  • All Siblings

    • ~

  • Not

    • :not
